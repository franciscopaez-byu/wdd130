RioVibe Adventures

Our mission is to transform an ordinary day into an epic water adventure, led by expert guides and world-class equipment. We connect people with the untamable force of nature

History

RioVibe Adventures was born in 1995 from the passion of two brothers, Francisco and Ramon, who spent their youth exploring the rapids of the Colorado Canyon.

What began with a single inflatable raft and an old truck has transformed today into the most trusted rafting company in the region.
